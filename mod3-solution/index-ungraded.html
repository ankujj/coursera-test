Chicken
I am Chicken. Chicken is considered white meat. Chicken is consumer all across the world and is easier to digest than other meats i.e. red meats. Chicken thighs are particulary juicy or so the people say. I would like to elaborate here on the way chicken is used in Indian Cusines. Some of the most popular Indian Chicken dishes include Butter Chicken, Chicken tandoori and Chicken Biryani. Butter Chicken is a not so spicy gravy contaning boneless chicken and is extremely rich in flavours. Chicken tandoori is made by marinating chicken in a mix of hung or greek youghurt, ginger garlic paste, indian spices, tandoori masala. It is preferred that the chicken stays marinated atlease overnight so that the marination seeps in the chicken making it more tasty and juicy. It is then grilled/baked in a Tandoor which also gives it a very smoky nice flavour. Chicken biryani, I am not sure exactly how it is made. However it is my spouse's favourite food. Back To Top
